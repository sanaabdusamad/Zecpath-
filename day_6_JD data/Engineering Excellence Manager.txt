Role Overview
The Engineering Excellence Manager drives engineering best practices, standards, and continuous improvement across the organization.
Key Responsibilities
• Define engineering standards, guidelines, and best practices
• Improve code quality, testing, and documentation
• Drive adoption of modern engineering practices
• Measure and improve engineering maturity
• Mentor teams on technical excellence
• Partner with leadership on improvement initiatives
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong engineering and quality background
• Experience driving process and technical improvements
• Metrics-driven improvement mindset
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Engineering quality metrics
• Reduction in defects and rework
• Adoption of best practices
