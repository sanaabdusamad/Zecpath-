Role Overview
The Engineering Team Manager is primarily responsible for people leadership, team health, and execution at the team level.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct one-on-one meetings and performance reviews
• Foster a positive, inclusive engineering culture
• Remove blockers and improve team productivity
• Coordinate daily operations and sprint execution
• Support hiring and onboarding
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong interpersonal and coaching skills
• Basic to intermediate technical understanding
• Conflict resolution and leadership skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Team morale and engagement
• Productivity and velocity
• Employee retention
