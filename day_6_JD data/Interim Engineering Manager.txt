Role Overview
An Interim Engineering Manager temporarily fills a leadership role during transitions, resignations, or organizational restructuring.
Key Responsibilities
• Maintain team stability and morale
• Continue delivery without disruption
• Manage ongoing projects and roadmaps
• Act as bridge between outgoing and incoming leadership
• Identify quick wins and operational improvements
• Support hiring and leadership transition
Required Skills & Experience
• 10+ years engineering management experience
• Strong crisis and change management skills
• Ability to adapt quickly to new environments
• Excellent people management and conflict resolution
Common Scenarios
• Leadership exits
• Mergers & acquisitions
• Organizational restructuring
