Role Overview
The Automotive Software Engineering Manager leads teams developing software for vehicles, infotainment, ADAS, and connected car platforms.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead development of automotive software systems
• Ensure compliance with automotive safety standards (ISO 26262)
• Oversee embedded and real-time software development
• Manage integration with hardware and vehicle systems
• Partner with product, hardware, and safety teams
• Drive quality, testing, and validation
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with embedded or automotive software
• Knowledge of safety-critical systems
• Strong system integration experience
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Software quality and safety compliance
• Release stability
• Integration success
