Role Overview
The Gaming Platform Engineering Manager leads teams building high-performance, scalable gaming platforms.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage backend and platform services for games
• Ensure low-latency and high-availability systems
• Support live game operations and scaling events
• Drive performance optimization and monitoring
• Collaborate with game developers and product teams
• Ensure secure and fair gameplay systems
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with real-time or high-traffic systems
• Knowledge of game backend architectures
• Strong performance engineering skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Platform uptime during peak load
• Latency and performance
• Player satisfaction
