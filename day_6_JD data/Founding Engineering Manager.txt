Role Overview
A Founding Engineering Manager is one of the first engineering leaders in a startup, responsible for building the initial product, team, and engineering culture from scratch.
Key Responsibilities
• Build and deliver the MVP and early platform
• Define system architecture and technical direction
• Hire, onboard, and mentor early engineers
• Set coding standards, DevOps practices, and tooling
• Work directly with founders on strategy and trade-offs
• Scale systems from prototype to production
• Own technical debt and long-term maintainability
Required Skills & Experience
• 8–12+ years engineering experience
• Proven early-stage startup background
• Strong system design and cloud architecture skills
• Ability to work with ambiguity and rapid change
• Leadership mindset with hands-on execution
