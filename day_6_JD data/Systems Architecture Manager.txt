Role Overview
The Systems Architecture Manager oversees complex, integrated systems architectures, often spanning software, infrastructure, and external integrations.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and govern end-to-end system architectures
• Ensure interoperability across systems and platforms
• Lead architecture for large-scale, mission-critical systems
• Manage system architects and senior engineers
• Drive performance, availability, and security improvements
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with large-scale system integration
• Strong understanding of infrastructure and networking
• Architectural modeling and documentation skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System reliability and performance
• Integration success rate
• Architecture scalability
