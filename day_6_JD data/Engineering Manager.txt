Role Overview
The Engineering Manager (EM) leads one or more engineering teams, balancing people management, technical oversight, and delivery excellence. The EM ensures high-quality software delivery while fostering team growth and collaboration.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead, mentor, and manage software engineers and tech leads
• Own team delivery, sprint planning, and execution
• Collaborate with Product, Design, QA, and DevOps teams
• Ensure engineering best practices, code quality, and system reliability
• Drive performance management, hiring, and team development
• Identify and mitigate technical and delivery risks
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong background in software engineering
• Experience with agile / scrum methodologies
• Leadership and stakeholder communication skills
• System design and architecture knowledge
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Delivery predictability
• Code quality and system stability
• Team engagement and retention
