Role Overview
A Consulting Engineering Manager works with client organizations to design, improve, and optimize engineering systems, processes, and teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Assess current engineering maturity and gaps
• Define target architecture and delivery models
• Lead transformation programs (Agile, DevOps, Cloud, AI)
• Guide engineering teams during execution
• Provide executive-level reporting and insights
• Ensure compliance with industry and security standards
Required Skills & Experience
• 10+ years in engineering leadership
• Strong consulting or enterprise delivery background
• Deep understanding of SDLC, DevOps, and cloud platforms
• Excellent presentation and client-facing skills
Industries Served
• Enterprise IT
• FinTech
• Healthcare
• Telecom
• Government & regulated sectors
