Role Overview
The Technical Program Engineering Manager leads complex, technically driven programs, ensuring alignment across architecture, engineering, and delivery teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Own planning and execution of large technical programs
• Manage technical dependencies and risks
• Coordinate across engineering, architecture, and product
• Track program milestones and outcomes
• Communicate progress to leadership
• Ensure technical alignment across teams
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong technical background with program leadership experience
• Ability to manage cross-team dependencies
• Excellent organizational and communication skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Program delivery success
• Dependency resolution effectiveness
• Technical alignment quality
