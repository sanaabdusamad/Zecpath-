Role Overview
The Chief Digital Engineering Officer leads digital transformation, modernization, and engineering innovation across the enterprise.
Key Responsibilities
• Drive digital engineering and transformation initiatives
• Lead modernization of legacy systems
• Oversee cloud, data, AI, and digital platforms
• Partner with business units to accelerate digital adoption
• Ensure scalable and future-ready engineering practices
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Experience in digital transformation leadership
• Strong background in cloud, data, and AI systems
• Enterprise change management expertise
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Digital transformation success
• Modernization ROI
• Business agility improvement
