Role Overview
The QA Engineering Manager leads quality assurance teams ensuring software quality, reliability, and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Define QA strategy and test approaches
• Lead manual and automation testing teams
• Ensure test coverage across functional and non-functional areas
• Integrate QA into CI/CD pipelines
• Drive quality metrics and reporting
• Partner with engineering and product teams
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong QA and testing background
• Test strategy and leadership experience
• Automation and CI/CD knowledge
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Defect leakage rate
• Test coverage
• Release quality
