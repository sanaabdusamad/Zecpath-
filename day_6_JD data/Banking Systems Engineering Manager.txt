Role Overview
The Banking Systems Engineering Manager leads teams building and maintaining core banking, digital banking, and transaction processing systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage development of core and digital banking platforms
• Ensure system reliability, security, and compliance
• Oversee integration with payment, AML, and fraud systems
• Support modernization of legacy banking systems
• Partner with risk, compliance, and business teams
• Lead incident response for critical banking systems
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with banking or financial systems
• Knowledge of regulatory and audit requirements
• Strong enterprise system experience
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System stability and uptime
• Regulatory audit success
• Transaction accuracy
