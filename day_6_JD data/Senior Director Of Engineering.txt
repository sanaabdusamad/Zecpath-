Role Overview
The Senior Director of Engineering provides executive-level leadership across large engineering organizations or portfolios.
Key Responsibilities
• Own engineering vision across multiple domains
• Scale engineering teams and processes
• Influence company-wide technology decisions
• Build leadership pipelines and succession plans
• Partner with C-level executives
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Proven experience leading large engineering orgs
• Strong business and financial acumen
• Ability to drive change at scale
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Organizational scalability
• Leadership effectiveness
• Business impact of engineering initiatives
