Role Overview
The Enterprise Engineering Manager leads engineering efforts across enterprise-wide platforms and systems, ensuring alignment with organizational standards and long-term strategy.
Key Responsibilities
• Own enterprise engineering standards and frameworks
• Manage large, cross-functional engineering teams
• Drive modernization of enterprise systems
• Ensure compliance, security, and governance
• Partner with business and IT leadership
• Oversee enterprise platform scalability
Required Skills & Experience
• Enterprise architecture and engineering experience
• Strong governance and compliance knowledge
• Experience managing large engineering programs
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Enterprise system modernization progress
• Platform stability and scalability
• Compliance adherence
