Role Overview
The DX Engineering Manager focuses on improving the daily experience of developers, enabling faster, happier, and more productive engineering teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Own developer tooling, workflows, and platforms
• Improve onboarding, build systems, and documentation
• Measure and optimize developer productivity metrics
• Partner with platform, DevOps, and engineering teams
• Drive adoption of internal tools and standards
• Advocate for developer needs at leadership level
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience in platform or tooling engineering
• Strong empathy for developer workflows
• Data-driven productivity improvement mindset
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Developer satisfaction scores
• Build and deployment times
• Onboarding efficiency
