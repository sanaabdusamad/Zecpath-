Role Overview
The Platform Engineering Manager leads teams building internal platforms, shared services, and developer tooling.
Key Responsibilities
• Own internal platform architecture and roadmap
• Improve developer experience and productivity
• Ensure scalability, reliability, and security
• Define APIs, standards, and shared services
• Collaborate with infrastructure and SRE teams
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Experience with cloud platforms and microservices
• Strong DevOps and automation knowledge
• System scalability expertise
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Platform reliability and performance
• Developer productivity improvements
• Adoption of platform services
