Role Overview
The Senior Engineering Manager leads multiple engineering teams or a large domain, owning delivery, technical direction, and people leadership at scale.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage multiple engineering managers or large teams
• Drive execution of complex, cross-team initiatives
• Align engineering delivery with product and business goals
• Own hiring strategy, performance management, and succession planning
• Ensure engineering best practices and scalable architecture
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong experience in engineering leadership
• System design and architectural decision-making
• Excellent stakeholder and executive communication
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Cross-team delivery success
• Engineering quality and scalability
• Leadership bench strength
