Role Overview
A Freelance Engineering Manager provides on-demand engineering leadership to startups or SMEs without long-term employment commitments.
Key Responsibilities
• Define technical execution plans aligned with business goals
• Lead distributed or remote engineering teams
• Set coding standards, architectural direction, and best practices
• Oversee sprint planning, estimation, and delivery
• Support hiring, onboarding, and team scaling
• Advise founders or executives on technical decisions
Required Skills & Experience
• 8+ years engineering experience
• Strong startup or fast-growth exposure
• Broad technical stack knowledge
• Ability to quickly understand business context
• Excellent remote leadership capabilities
Engagement Models
• Hourly / monthly retainers
• Part-time leadership
• Milestone-based delivery
