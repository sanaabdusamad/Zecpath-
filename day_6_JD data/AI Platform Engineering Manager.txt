Role Overview
The AI Platform Engineering Manager leads teams building and operating shared AI/ML platforms that enable scalable model development, deployment, and governance across the organization.
Key Responsibilities
• Own AI platform architecture (model serving, pipelines, feature stores)
• Lead teams building reusable AI infrastructure and APIs
• Ensure scalability, reliability, and performance of AI systems
• Partner with data science, product, and MLOps teams
• Drive standardization of AI tooling and workflows
• Ensure security, compliance, and cost optimization
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong background in platform or backend engineering
• Experience with ML platforms, cloud AI services, MLOps
• People leadership and cross-team collaboration
• Knowledge of distributed systems and APIs
KPIs / Success Metrics
• AI platform adoption
• Model deployment velocity
• Platform reliability and cost efficiency
