Role Overview
The Solutions Engineering Manager leads teams that design and deliver technical solutions for customers or internal stakeholders, bridging business needs and engineering execution.
Key Responsibilities
• Translate business and customer requirements into technical solutions
• Lead solution design and technical proposals
• Manage solution engineers and architects
• Partner with sales, product, and delivery teams
• Ensure feasibility, scalability, and security of solutions
• Support pre-sales and technical enablement
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong solution architecture and design experience
• Customer-facing and stakeholder management skills
• Broad technical knowledge across systems and platforms
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Solution success and adoption
• Customer satisfaction
• Delivery feasibility and quality
