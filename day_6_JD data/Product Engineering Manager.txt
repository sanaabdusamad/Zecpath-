Role Overview
The Product Engineering Manager bridges product strategy and engineering execution, ensuring customer-centric and scalable solutions.
Key Responsibilities
• Partner closely with Product Managers
• Translate product requirements into technical solutions
• Prioritize features based on business impact
• Ensure high-quality, user-focused delivery
• Balance innovation with technical sustainability
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong product mindset
• Experience with customer-facing systems
• Ability to make trade-off decisions
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Feature adoption
• Customer satisfaction
• Time-to-market
