Role Overview
The Director of Engineering owns engineering strategy, delivery, and execution across multiple product or platform areas.
Key Responsibilities
• Define engineering strategy aligned with business objectives
• Lead multiple engineering teams and managers
• Manage budgets, resource planning, and capacity
• Partner with Product, Design, and Business leadership
• Drive operational excellence and delivery predictability
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong organizational leadership experience
• Experience managing managers
• Budgeting and strategic planning skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Engineering throughput
• Cost efficiency
• Product delivery outcomes
