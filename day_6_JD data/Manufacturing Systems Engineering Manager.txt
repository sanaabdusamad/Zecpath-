Role Overview
The Manufacturing Systems Engineering Manager leads engineering teams building industrial, factory, and manufacturing execution systems (MES).
Key Responsibilities
• Manage development of manufacturing systems and platforms
• Integrate IT systems with industrial equipment and IoT
• Ensure system reliability and real-time data processing
• Support automation and digital manufacturing initiatives
• Collaborate with operations and plant teams
• Ensure safety and compliance
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with manufacturing or industrial systems
• Knowledge of automation, IoT, or MES platforms
• Strong system integration skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System uptime and reliability
• Production efficiency improvement
• Integration success
