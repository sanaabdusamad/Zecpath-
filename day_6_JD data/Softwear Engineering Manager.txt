Role Overview
The Software Engineering Manager oversees software development teams responsible for building scalable, secure, and maintainable applications.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage end-to-end software development lifecycle
• Guide technical design and architecture decisions
• Ensure adherence to coding standards and SDLC practices
• Coordinate releases and deployments
• Support engineers’ career growth and performance
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Proficiency in backend, frontend, or full-stack development
• Experience with CI/CD pipelines
• Strong debugging and problem-solving skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Release quality and velocity
• Defect rate and post-release stability
• Team skill advancement
