Role Overview
A Player–Coach Engineering Manager combines deep technical contribution with active mentorship and leadership, especially in lean teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Act as lead contributor on critical features
• Coach engineers through pairing, reviews, and design sessions
• Set technical standards and development practices
• Guide junior and mid-level engineers
• Own delivery timelines and quality outcomes
• Support hiring and onboarding
Required Skills & Experience
• 7–12+ years engineering experience
• Strong mentoring and coaching mindset
• Hands-on expertise in architecture and coding
• Experience leading small, high-impact teams
