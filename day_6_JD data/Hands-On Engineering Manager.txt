Role Overview
A Hands-On Engineering Manager actively contributes code while also managing people, delivery, and technical direction.
Key Responsibilities
• Write, review, and maintain production-grade code
• Lead sprint planning, estimations, and delivery
• Provide technical guidance and unblock engineers
• Manage team performance and career growth
• Ensure code quality, testing, and CI/CD practices
• Balance management duties with development work
Required Skills & Experience
• 6–10+ years engineering experience
• Strong coding expertise in relevant tech stack
• Prior Tech Lead or hybrid leadership experience
• Ability to context-switch effectively
