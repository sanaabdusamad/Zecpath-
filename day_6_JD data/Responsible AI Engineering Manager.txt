Role Overview
The Responsible AI Engineering Manager ensures that AI systems are ethical, fair, explainable, and compliant, embedding responsible AI principles into engineering practices.
Key Responsibilities
• Implement fairness, bias detection, and explainability frameworks
• Lead responsible AI engineering standards and reviews
• Ensure compliance with AI regulations and internal policies
• Collaborate with legal, compliance, and data teams
• Drive transparency and auditability of AI systems
• Educate teams on ethical AI best practices
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong understanding of AI ethics and governance
• Experience with ML systems and compliance frameworks
• Stakeholder management and policy implementation skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Compliance audit outcomes
• Reduction in AI risk incidents
• Explainability and transparency metrics
