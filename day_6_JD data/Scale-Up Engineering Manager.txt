Role Overview
A Scale-Up Engineering Manager focuses on growing teams, systems, and processes as the company scales from startup to mid-size or enterprise.
Key Responsibilities
• Scale engineering teams and delivery capacity
• Redesign systems for performance, reliability, and growth
• Introduce structured Agile, DevOps, and QA processes
• Manage hiring, performance, and team structure
• Reduce technical debt and improve platform stability
• Support organizational and cultural scaling
Required Skills & Experience
• 8–12+ years engineering experience
• Experience scaling systems and teams
• Strong cloud, distributed systems knowledge
• Proven people management and hiring experience
