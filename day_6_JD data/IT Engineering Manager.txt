Role Overview
The IT Engineering Manager leads IT systems, infrastructure, and enterprise technology operations, ensuring business continuity and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage IT engineering and support teams
• Oversee enterprise infrastructure and systems
• Ensure security, compliance, and availability
• Plan IT upgrades and capacity
• Align IT initiatives with business goals
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Experience in IT infrastructure and operations
• Knowledge of ITSM, ITIL, and security standards
• Vendor and budget management skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System availability
• Incident response time
• IT cost optimization
