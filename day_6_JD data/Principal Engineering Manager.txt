Role Overview
The Principal Engineering Manager combines deep technical authority with organizational leadership, influencing architecture and engineering culture across teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and govern technical standards and architecture
• Mentor senior engineers and engineering managers
• Drive long-term technical vision and roadmap
• Lead critical, high-impact engineering initiatives
• Reduce technical debt and improve engineering maturity
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Expert-level system architecture experience
• Strong influence without direct authority
• Proven record of solving large-scale technical challenges
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Architectural consistency
• Platform scalability
• Reduction of technical risk
