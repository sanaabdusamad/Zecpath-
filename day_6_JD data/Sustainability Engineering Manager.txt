Role Overview
The Sustainability Engineering Manager leads initiatives to reduce the environmental impact of technology systems while aligning engineering practices with ESG goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Design energy-efficient systems and architectures
• Measure and reduce carbon footprint of IT operations
• Drive sustainable cloud and infrastructure practices
• Partner with ESG, compliance, and leadership teams
• Promote green software engineering principles
• Report sustainability metrics and outcomes
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience in infrastructure, cloud, or systems engineering
• Knowledge of sustainability metrics and ESG frameworks
• Leadership experience in cross-functional initiatives
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Carbon footprint reduction
• Energy efficiency improvements
• Sustainability compliance metrics
