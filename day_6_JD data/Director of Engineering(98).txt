Role Overview
The Director of Engineering provides org-wide technical and people leadership, translating business strategy into scalable engineering execution.
Key Responsibilities
• Define engineering strategy and execution plans
• Lead multiple engineering orgs or functions
• Own budgeting, hiring, and capacity planning
• Drive platform, architecture, and quality standards
• Partner with executives on product and technology strategy
• Manage senior leaders and succession planning
• Ensure operational excellence and risk management
Required Skills & Experience
• 12–18+ years engineering experience
• Strong leadership of leaders
• Experience scaling teams and systems
• Business and financial acumen
Success Metrics
• Engineering org effectiveness
• Delivery of strategic initiatives
• Talent retention and leadership pipeline
