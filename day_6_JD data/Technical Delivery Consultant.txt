Role Overview
A Technical Delivery Consultant ensures that complex technical programs are delivered successfully across multiple teams and stakeholders.
Key Responsibilities
• Own end-to-end technical delivery for client engagements
• Align engineering execution with business timelines
• Manage dependencies across teams and vendors
• Identify delivery risks and mitigation strategies
• Ensure quality, performance, and security standards
• Report delivery status to senior leadership
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong engineering background with delivery ownership
• Experience managing large-scale or multi-team programs
• Excellent planning, estimation, and reporting skills
• Strong stakeholder management abilities
Focus Areas
• Large platform implementations
• Cloud migrations
• Digital transformation programs
