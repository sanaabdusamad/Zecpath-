Role Overview
A Contract Engineering Manager is engaged for a fixed duration to lead engineering teams, stabilize delivery, and execute specific technical or transformation initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead one or more engineering teams during the contract period
• Ensure timely delivery of committed milestones
• Provide hands-on technical guidance and architectural reviews
• Improve team processes, quality standards, and delivery velocity
• Collaborate with product, business, and vendor teams
• Manage technical risks and dependencies
• Mentor internal engineers and hand over knowledge before exit
Required Skills & Experience
• 8–12+ years in software/engineering roles
• Proven experience as Engineering Manager or Tech Lead
• Strong system design and code review expertise
• Agile/Scrum delivery experience
• Excellent stakeholder communication skills
Typical Use Cases
• Scale-up phases
• Leadership gaps
• Program rescue or turnaround
• Platform modernization initiatives
