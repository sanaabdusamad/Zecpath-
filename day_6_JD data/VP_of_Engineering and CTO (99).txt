Role Overview
The VP of Engineering / CTO is the top technology leader, responsible for vision, strategy, innovation, and long-term technical competitiveness.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and own the company’s technology vision and roadmap
• Align technology strategy with business objectives
• Lead all engineering, architecture, and platform teams
• Make executive-level decisions on architecture and investments
• Drive innovation, security, and technical excellence
• Represent technology to the board, customers, and partners
• Build strong engineering culture and leadership bench
Required Skills & Experience
• 15–25+ years of technology leadership experience
• Proven experience leading large engineering organizations
• Deep technical background with strategic vision
• Strong executive communication and decision-making skills
Success Metrics
• Business impact of technology strategy
• Platform scalability and innovation
• Organizational health and leadership strength
