Role Overview
The Release Engineering Manager leads teams responsible for build, release, and deployment processes, ensuring reliable and repeatable releases.
Key Responsibilities
• Own release planning and coordination
• Automate build and release pipelines
• Ensure release quality and stability
• Manage release risks and rollback strategies
• Coordinate across engineering, QA, and operations
• Drive continuous improvement of release processes
Required Skills & Experience
• CI/CD and automation expertise
• Release management experience
• Strong cross-team coordination skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Release success rate
• Deployment frequency
• Change failure rate
