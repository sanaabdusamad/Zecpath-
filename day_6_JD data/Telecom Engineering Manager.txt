Role Overview
The Telecom Engineering Manager leads engineering teams building telecommunications platforms and network systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage development of telecom applications and platforms
• Ensure high availability and performance of network systems
• Oversee OSS/BSS systems and integrations
• Drive automation and system scalability
• Collaborate with network operations teams
• Ensure compliance with telecom standards
Required Skills & Experience
• Telecom systems and networking knowledge
• Experience with large-scale distributed systems
• Strong operational leadership
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Network availability
• Incident resolution time
• Platform scalability
