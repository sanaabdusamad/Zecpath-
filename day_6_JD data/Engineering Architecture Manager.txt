Role Overview
The Engineering Architecture Manager leads teams responsible for defining, governing, and evolving system architecture across products and platforms while managing senior engineers and architects.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and enforce architectural standards and principles
• Lead architecture reviews and technical design governance
• Guide teams on scalability, security, and performance
• Balance innovation with long-term maintainability
• Mentor architects and senior engineers
• Align architecture decisions with business strategy
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong system and solution architecture background
• Experience with distributed and cloud-native systems
• Proven people leadership experience
• Excellent technical communication skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Architectural consistency and compliance
• Scalability and reliability outcomes
• Reduction of architectural debt
