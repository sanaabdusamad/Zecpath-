Role Overview
The E-Commerce Engineering Manager leads teams building online commerce platforms, ensuring scalability, performance, and conversion optimization.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead development of e-commerce applications and services
• Ensure performance during high-traffic events
• Integrate payment, catalog, and logistics systems
• Drive A/B testing and optimization
• Partner with product, marketing, and operations teams
• Ensure security and compliance for transactions
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with e-commerce platforms
• High-traffic system scalability expertise
• Payment and order management knowledge
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Conversion rate and checkout success
• Platform uptime
• Page load performance
