Role Overview
The Performance Engineering Manager ensures systems meet performance, scalability, and reliability requirements.
Key Responsibilities
• Define performance testing strategy
• Conduct load, stress, and scalability testing
• Identify bottlenecks and optimization opportunities
• Collaborate with engineering teams on tuning
• Monitor performance in production
• Establish performance benchmarks
Required Skills & Experience
• Performance testing tools and techniques
• System architecture understanding
• Monitoring and observability expertise
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System response times
• Throughput and scalability metrics
• Performance defect reduction
