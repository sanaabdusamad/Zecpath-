Role Overview
A Fractional Engineering Manager provides part-time engineering leadership to startups or growing companies that don’t yet need a full-time EM.
Key Responsibilities
• Define technical vision and execution roadmap
• Lead engineering best practices and standards
• Coach tech leads and senior engineers
• Support architecture, hiring, and performance reviews
• Act as strategic advisor to founders and leadership
• Ensure scalability and technical sustainability
Required Skills & Experience
• 10+ years in engineering leadership
• Strong startup or scale-up experience
• Broad architectural and system design expertise
• Ability to balance strategy with hands-on guidance
Ideal For
• Early-stage startups
• Growing product companies
• Cost-conscious organizations
