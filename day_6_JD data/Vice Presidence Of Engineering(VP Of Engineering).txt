Role Overview
The VP of Engineering leads the entire engineering organization, ensuring technology execution supports business growth and innovation.
Key Responsibilities
• Define long-term engineering and technology strategy
• Own engineering budgets, staffing, and operations
• Partner with CEO, CTO, and Product leadership
• Drive innovation, scalability, and engineering excellence
• Represent engineering at the executive level
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Extensive experience leading engineering at scale
• Strong strategic and business mindset
• Experience in high-growth or enterprise environments
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Engineering ROI
• Product delivery velocity
• Technology scalability and reliability
