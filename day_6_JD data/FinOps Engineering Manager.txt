Role Overview
The FinOps Engineering Manager leads engineering efforts focused on cloud cost optimization, financial governance, and efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Own cloud cost optimization and budgeting initiatives
• Implement cost monitoring, alerting, and governance tools
• Partner with finance, procurement, and engineering teams
• Drive efficient resource usage and architecture decisions
• Educate teams on cost-aware engineering practices
• Forecast and manage cloud spend
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong cloud infrastructure and cost management experience
• FinOps frameworks and financial modeling knowledge
• Cross-functional collaboration skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Cloud cost savings
• Budget adherence
• Cost efficiency per workload
