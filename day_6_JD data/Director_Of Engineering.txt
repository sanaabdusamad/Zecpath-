Role Overview
The Director of Engineering owns engineering strategy, execution, and organizational effectiveness across multiple domains, products, or platforms.
This role focuses on scaling teams, processes, and systems while aligning engineering output with business outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and execute engineering strategy aligned with business goals
• Lead multiple Senior Engineering Managers and Engineering Managers
• Own capacity planning, budgeting, and resource allocation
• Establish engineering processes, metrics, and governance
• Drive operational excellence and delivery consistency
• Partner with executive leadership on roadmap planning
• Build a strong leadership pipeline within engineering
Required Skills & Experience
• 12–18+ years of engineering experience
• Proven experience managing large engineering organizations
• Strong business, budgeting, and planning skills
• Experience working closely with executive stakeholders
• Ability to lead organizational change
Success Metrics (KPIs)
• Engineering throughput and cost efficiency
• Delivery success across portfolios
• Leadership bench strength
• Platform and product scalability
