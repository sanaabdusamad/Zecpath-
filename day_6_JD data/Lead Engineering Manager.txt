Role Overview
A Lead Engineering Manager oversees multiple teams or senior engineers, acting as a bridge between execution, architecture, and leadership.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead multiple squads or a large engineering pod
• Guide technical decisions and architectural alignment
• Mentor Engineering Managers and Tech Leads
• Coordinate cross-team dependencies and releases
• Ensure engineering best practices and consistency
• Partner with product leadership on roadmap planning
Required Skills & Experience
• 8–14+ years engineering experience
• Prior Engineering Manager or Tech Lead experience
• Strong leadership and cross-team coordination skills
• Excellent system design and review capabilities
