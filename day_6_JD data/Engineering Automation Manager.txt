Role Overview
The Engineering Automation Manager drives automation across the software development lifecycle, improving speed, quality, and reliability.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead automation of CI/CD, testing, and infrastructure
• Reduce manual engineering effort through tooling
• Implement self-service engineering platforms
• Collaborate with DevOps and platform teams
• Measure efficiency gains and quality improvements
• Promote automation-first engineering culture
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong DevOps, CI/CD, and automation background
• Experience with infrastructure as code and testing automation
• Leadership experience in tooling teams
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Automation coverage
• Deployment frequency
• Reduction in manual errors
