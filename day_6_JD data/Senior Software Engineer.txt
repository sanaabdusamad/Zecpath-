Role Overview
A Senior Software Engineer is a highly experienced individual contributor responsible for designing, building, and maintaining complex software systems while mentoring others.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and implement scalable, high-quality software solutions
• Own end-to-end delivery of complex features or services
• Perform code reviews and enforce engineering best practices
• Debug production issues and improve system reliability
• Collaborate with product, QA, and DevOps teams
• Mentor junior and mid-level engineers
• Contribute to architectural discussions
Required Skills & Experience
• 5–8+ years of professional software development experience
• Strong expertise in one or more programming languages
• Experience with system design, APIs, databases, and cloud
• Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
Success Metrics
• Code quality and system stability
• Feature delivery reliability
• Mentorship impact
