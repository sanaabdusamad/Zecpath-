Role Overview
A Senior Engineering Manager leads multiple teams or managers, focusing on scalability, cross-team alignment, and long-term execution.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and mentor Engineering Managers and senior leads
• Own delivery across multiple teams or domains
• Align engineering execution with business goals
• Drive architectural consistency and platform strategy
• Manage hiring plans and org design
• Handle escalations and complex delivery risks
• Partner with senior product and business leaders
Required Skills & Experience
• 10–15+ years engineering experience
• Proven multi-team leadership experience
• Strong organizational and strategic thinking
• Excellent stakeholder management
Success Metrics
• Cross-team execution success
• Organizational scalability
• Engineering leadership maturity
