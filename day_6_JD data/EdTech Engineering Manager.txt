Role Overview
The EdTech Engineering Manager leads teams building digital learning platforms and education technology solutions.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage development of learning platforms and tools
• Ensure scalability for large user bases
• Support content delivery and analytics
• Collaborate with educators and product teams
• Drive accessibility and usability
• Ensure data privacy for learners
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with SaaS or platform engineering
• User-centric product development mindset
• Knowledge of data privacy regulations
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Platform engagement
• System reliability
• Learning outcome support
