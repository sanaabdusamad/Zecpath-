Role Overview
The Senior Engineering Manager (SEM) leads multiple engineering teams or a large product/platform area, owning delivery excellence, people leadership, and technical alignment across teams.
This role sits between execution and strategy and is critical for scaling engineering organizations.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and manage multiple engineering teams or Engineering Managers
• Own delivery of complex, cross-team initiatives
• Translate business and product goals into engineering execution plans
• Ensure high engineering standards, code quality, and architectural alignment
• Drive hiring, performance management, coaching, and succession planning
• Identify risks, dependencies, and bottlenecks early and resolve them
• Partner with Product, Design, QA, and DevOps leadership
Required Skills & Experience
• 10–14+ years of engineering experience
• 3–6+ years in engineering leadership roles
• Strong system design and architectural understanding
• Experience managing managers and senior engineers
• Excellent stakeholder communication skills
Success Metrics (KPIs)
• Cross-team delivery predictability
• Engineering quality and system stability
• Team engagement and retention
• Reduction of operational and technical risks
