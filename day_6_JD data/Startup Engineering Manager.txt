Role Overview
A Startup Engineering Manager leads small, fast-moving engineering teams in an early-stage or growth-stage startup, balancing delivery, people leadership, and technical execution.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead day-to-day engineering execution across product features
• Translate business goals into technical roadmaps
• Manage sprint planning, prioritization, and delivery
• Collaborate closely with founders, product, and design
• Establish engineering processes, standards, and culture
• Mentor engineers and support hiring decisions
• Ensure scalability, performance, and reliability
Required Skills & Experience
• 6–10+ years in software engineering
• Prior startup or fast-growth experience
• Strong full-stack or platform engineering background
• Agile / Lean delivery experience
• Excellent problem-solving and ownership mindset
