Role Overview
The Healthcare Engineering Manager leads teams developing healthcare software systems, ensuring patient data security, compliance, and system reliability.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage development of healthcare applications and platforms
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations (HIPAA, HL7, etc.)
• Oversee integration with clinical and medical systems
• Drive data privacy and security best practices
• Collaborate with clinicians, product, and compliance teams
• Ensure high availability of critical healthcare systems
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience in healthcare or regulated environments
• Strong data privacy and security knowledge
• Experience with system integrations
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Regulatory compliance
• System uptime
• Data security incidents
