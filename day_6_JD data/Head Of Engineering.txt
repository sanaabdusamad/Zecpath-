Role Overview
The Head of Engineering is accountable for end-to-end engineering execution, often in startups or mid-size organizations.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and scale engineering teams
• Set engineering standards and processes
• Align engineering with product and business goals
• Manage hiring, performance, and culture
• Ensure system stability and innovation
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Hands-on engineering leadership experience
• Ability to scale teams and systems
• Strong cross-functional leadership
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Time-to-market
• Team growth and retention
• System reliability
