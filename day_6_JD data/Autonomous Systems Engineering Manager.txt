Role Overview
The Autonomous Systems Engineering Manager leads engineering teams developing self-governing, decision-making systems such as robotics, autonomous agents, and AI-driven control systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead design and development of autonomous software systems
• Manage safety, reliability, and fail-safe mechanisms
• Coordinate simulation, testing, and real-world deployment
• Collaborate with AI, hardware, and control systems teams
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Guide system scalability and autonomy maturity
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience in autonomous systems, robotics, or AI agents
• Strong systems engineering and control theory understanding
• Leadership experience in complex, safety-critical systems
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Autonomy performance and stability
• Safety incident reduction
• System decision accuracy
