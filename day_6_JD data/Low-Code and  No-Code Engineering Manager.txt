Role Overview
The Low-Code / No-Code Engineering Manager leads platforms that enable rapid application development by business and technical users with minimal hand-coded effort.
Key Responsibilities
• Own low-code/no-code platform strategy and governance
• Ensure scalability, security, and maintainability of solutions
• Manage platform engineering and enablement teams
• Define development standards and guardrails
• Partner with business units and IT governance
• Drive adoption and developer enablement
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience with low-code platforms and enterprise systems
• Strong governance and platform management skills
• Ability to balance speed with quality and security
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Platform adoption rates
• Time-to-delivery improvement
• Reduction in shadow IT
