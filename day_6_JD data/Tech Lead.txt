Role Overview
A Tech Lead is the technical decision-maker for a team, guiding architecture, standards, and execution while remaining hands-on.
Key Responsibilities
• Define technical design and architecture for team initiatives
• Lead technical planning, estimations, and execution
• Review code and ensure engineering excellence
• Mentor engineers and raise team technical maturity
• Act as technical interface to product and leadership
• Resolve complex technical challenges
• Ensure scalability, performance, and security
Required Skills & Experience
• 7–10+ years of engineering experience
• Strong system design and architectural expertise
• Leadership without direct people management (in many orgs)
• Excellent communication and decision-making skills
Success Metrics
• Technical quality and architecture health
• Delivery predictability
• Team technical growth
