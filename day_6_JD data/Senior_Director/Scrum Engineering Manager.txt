Role Overview
The Scrum Engineering Manager combines scrum leadership with engineering management, ensuring disciplined execution and team health.
Key Responsibilities
• Facilitate scrum ceremonies (planning, reviews, retrospectives)
• Support backlog refinement and sprint planning
• Remove impediments and protect team focus
• Ensure adherence to scrum best practices
• Mentor engineers and scrum leads
• Coordinate with Product Owners
Required Skills & Experience
• Scrum framework expertise
• Engineering or technical background
• Strong facilitation and communication skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Sprint goal achievement
• Team velocity stability
• Scrum process adherence
