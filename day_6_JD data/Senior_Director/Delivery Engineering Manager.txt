Role Overview
The Delivery Engineering Manager owns end-to-end delivery execution, ensuring predictable, on-time, and high-quality software releases.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and track engineering delivery commitments
• Manage dependencies, risks, and timelines
• Improve delivery predictability and velocity
• Coordinate across product, engineering, QA, and DevOps
• Establish delivery metrics and reporting
• Drive continuous delivery improvements
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong project and delivery management skills
• Agile / Scrum / Kanban expertise
• Stakeholder and risk management experience
KPIs / Success Metrics
• On-time delivery rate
• Sprint predictability
• Stakeholder satisfaction
