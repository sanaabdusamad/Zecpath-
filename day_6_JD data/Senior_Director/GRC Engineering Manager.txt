Role Overview
Leads Governance, Risk, and Compliance engineering initiatives ensuring regulatory and security compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Implement compliance frameworks (ISO, SOC, GDPR, etc.)
• Manage risk assessments and controls
• Coordinate audits and remediation
• Align engineering practices with regulations
• Report compliance status to leadership
Required Skills & Experience
• GRC frameworks and standards
• Risk management expertise
• Cross-functional leadership skills
KPIs
• Audit success rate
• Risk reduction
• Compliance maturity
