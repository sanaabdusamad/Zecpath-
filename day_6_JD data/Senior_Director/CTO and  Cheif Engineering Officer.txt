Role Overview
The CTO / Chief Engineering Officer is responsible for the company’s overall technology and engineering vision, ensuring long-term innovation, competitiveness, and execution excellence.
This role blends strategy, architecture, innovation, and executive leadership.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and own the company’s technology and engineering vision
• Drive innovation, R&D, and adoption of emerging technologies
• Align engineering strategy with business growth and market needs
• Oversee architecture, platforms, security, and technical governance
• Mentor senior engineering and technology leaders
• Represent technology to investors, partners, and external stakeholders
• Ensure long-term scalability, resilience, and sustainability
Required Skills & Experience
• 18–25+ years of deep technical and leadership experience
• Strong background in system architecture and modern platforms
• Proven executive leadership at enterprise or high-growth scale
• Business, financial, and strategic planning expertise
• Visionary mindset with execution discipline
Success Metrics (KPIs)
• Technology-driven business growth
• Innovation impact and competitive advantage
• Platform scalability and modernization success
• Engineering culture and leadership maturity
