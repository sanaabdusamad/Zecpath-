Role Overview
The Program Engineering Manager leads large, cross-team engineering programs, aligning multiple teams toward shared objectives.
Key Responsibilities
• Own planning and execution of multi-team programs
• Manage scope, dependencies, and risks
• Align technical execution with business priorities
• Coordinate across engineering, product, and leadership
• Track program milestones and outcomes
• Drive program governance and communication
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience managing large technical programs
• Strong coordination and leadership skills
• Understanding of system dependencies
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Program delivery success
• Dependency resolution effectiveness
• Business outcome achievement
