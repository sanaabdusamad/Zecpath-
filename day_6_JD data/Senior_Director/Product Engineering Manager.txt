Role Overview
The Product Engineering Manager bridges product vision and engineering execution, ensuring customer-focused, scalable, and high-quality product delivery.
Key Responsibilities
• Partner closely with Product Managers and UX teams
• Translate product requirements into technical solutions
• Prioritize features based on customer and business impact
• Ensure engineering quality, scalability, and maintainability
• Balance speed of delivery with long-term technical health
• Lead and mentor product-aligned engineering teams
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong software engineering background
• Product-centric mindset
• Experience with customer-facing systems
• Agile product development experience
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Feature adoption and customer satisfaction
• Time-to-market
• Product quality and stability
