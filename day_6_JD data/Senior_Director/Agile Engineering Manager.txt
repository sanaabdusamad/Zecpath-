Role Overview
The Agile Engineering Manager ensures teams adopt and continuously improve agile practices while maintaining engineering excellence.
Key Responsibilities
• Coach teams on agile principles and practices
• Improve team velocity, predictability, and collaboration
• Remove delivery blockers and process inefficiencies
• Partner with product and engineering leaders
• Scale agile practices across teams
• Measure and improve agile maturity
Required Skills & Experience
• Deep agile and lean methodology knowledge
• Engineering leadership background
• Strong coaching and facilitation skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Agile maturity improvement
• Delivery consistency
• Team engagement
