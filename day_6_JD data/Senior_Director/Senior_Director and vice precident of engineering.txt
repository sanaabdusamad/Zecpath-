Role Overview
The Senior Director / VP of Engineering leads enterprise or company-wide engineering execution, ensuring technology delivery supports growth, innovation, and long-term scalability.
This is a top-tier executive leadership role with direct influence on company performance.
Key Responsibilities
• Own engineering execution across all products and platforms
• Define long-term engineering vision and operating model
• Manage large-scale budgets, headcount, and vendor relationships
• Partner with CEO, CTO, Product, and Business leaders
• Drive engineering transformation, modernization, and innovation
• Represent engineering in executive and board-level discussions
• Ensure reliability, security, and compliance at scale
Required Skills & Experience
• 15–22+ years of experience in engineering leadership
• Proven success leading large, multi-location engineering orgs
• Strong strategic, financial, and operational acumen
• Experience scaling systems and teams in high-growth environments
• Exceptional executive communication skills
Success Metrics (KPIs)
• Engineering ROI and business impact
• Organizational scalability and efficiency
• Technology reliability and delivery velocity
• Successful execution of transformation initiatives
