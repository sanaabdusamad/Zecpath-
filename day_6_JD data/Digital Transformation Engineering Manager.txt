Role Overview
The Digital Transformation Engineering Manager leads modernization initiatives, transforming legacy systems into cloud-native, data-driven platforms.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead legacy modernization and cloud migration programs
• Drive adoption of modern architectures and tooling
• Manage cross-functional transformation teams
• Align transformation efforts with business strategy
• Measure ROI and transformation outcomes
• Manage technical risk and change management
Required Skills & Experience
• Experience in large-scale digital transformation
• Cloud, microservices, and modernization expertise
• Strong stakeholder and program leadership skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Modernization progress
• System performance improvements
• Business value realization
