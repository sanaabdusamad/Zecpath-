Role Overview
The Chief Engineering Officer provides enterprise-wide engineering leadership, focusing on execution excellence and technical governance.
Key Responsibilities
• Own engineering execution across all business units
• Establish engineering governance and standards
• Oversee delivery, quality, and operational excellence
• Collaborate with CTO and business leaders
• Drive engineering transformation initiatives
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Executive-level engineering leadership experience
• Strong operational and governance expertise
• Enterprise systems and delivery knowledge
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Engineering effectiveness
• Delivery predictability
• Compliance and quality metrics
