Role Overview
The Development Manager focuses on planning, execution, and delivery of development projects while managing development teams.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan development roadmaps and timelines
• Allocate resources across projects
• Monitor progress, risks, and dependencies
• Ensure timely delivery of features
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong project and people management skills
• Experience with agile and waterfall models
• Knowledge of SDLC tools (Jira, Azure DevOps, etc.)
KPIs / Success Metrics
• On-time delivery
• Resource utilization
• Stakeholder satisfaction
