Role Overview
The Systems Engineering Manager oversees complex, integrated systems, often across hardware, software, and infrastructure.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage system design and integration
• Ensure system reliability, performance, and security
• Coordinate cross-team dependencies
• Oversee incident management and root-cause analysis
• Drive continuous system improvements
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Experience with large-scale systems
• Knowledge of networking, OS, and distributed systems
• Strong troubleshooting skills
KPIs / Success Metrics
• System uptime and availability
• Incident frequency and resolution time
• Performance benchmarks
