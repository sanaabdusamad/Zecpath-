Role Overview
The FinTech Engineering Manager leads engineering teams building secure, scalable financial technology platforms such as payments, lending, wallets, and trading systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead development of financial systems and services
• Ensure compliance with financial regulations and standards
• Drive security, reliability, and performance of transaction systems
• Partner with product, risk, and compliance teams
• Oversee integrations with banks, payment gateways, and third parties
• Manage high-availability and low-latency systems
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong backend or platform engineering background
• Experience with financial systems and payments
• Knowledge of security, compliance, and audit requirements
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Transaction success and latency
• Security and compliance adherence
• System availability
