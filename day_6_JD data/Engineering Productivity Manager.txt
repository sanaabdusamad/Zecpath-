Role Overview
The Engineering Productivity Manager focuses on improving developer efficiency, tooling, and workflows to maximize engineering output.
Key Responsibilities
• Identify productivity bottlenecks in engineering workflows
• Own developer tooling, CI/CD, and automation initiatives
• Improve onboarding and development environments
• Measure and optimize developer experience (DX)
• Partner with platform, DevOps, and engineering teams
• Drive adoption of productivity tools and practices
Required Skills & Experience
• Strong background in developer tooling and platforms
• DevOps and automation expertise
• Experience measuring and improving DX
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Developer productivity metrics
• Build and deployment times
• Tool adoption and satisfaction
