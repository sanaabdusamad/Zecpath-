Role Overview
The CTO defines the company’s technology vision and innovation strategy, ensuring long-term technical competitiveness.
Key Responsibilities
• Define technology roadmap and architecture vision
• Drive innovation, R&D, and emerging technologies
• Align technology strategy with business goals
• Represent technology to investors and stakeholders
• Guide senior engineering and architecture leaders
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Deep technical and architectural expertise
• Strong strategic and visionary leadership
• Experience with emerging technologies
KPIs / Success Metrics
• Technology innovation impact
• Platform scalability and modernization
• Competitive differentiation
